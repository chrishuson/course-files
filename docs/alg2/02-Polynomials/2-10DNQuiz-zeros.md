$$x(x-5)(x+2)=0$$

    a. $f(5)=0$
    b. $f$ has degree 3.
    c. One of the zeros of $f$ is 9.
    d. An ordered pair satisfying the equation is $(-1,0)$
    e. $f(0)=0$
